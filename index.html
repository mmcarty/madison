Madison is an AI-hosted live streaming show. This site covers the companion posting tool we operate to publish our own short promotional clips to our own social accounts — including TikTok — to promote the show.

What the posting tool does

The tool runs on our own machines. It takes short promotional video clips we produce for the show and publishes them to social accounts we own and control, with a caption we set for each clip.

On TikTok, it uses the official Content Posting API to publish those clips to our own authorized TikTok account. It does not act on behalf of anyone else, and it is not offered as a service to other users.
